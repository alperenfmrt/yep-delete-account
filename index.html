TechnoHub Account Deletion

TechnoHub allows users to permanently delete their accounts and associated personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the TechnoHub app.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion request.
  4. Your account will be permanently deleted.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Profile information
  • Email address
  • Messages
  • Marketplace listings
  • Favorites
  • Uploaded images associated with your account

Data Retention

Some information may be retained temporarily if required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, or enforce our Terms of Service. Any retained data will be deleted once it is no longer required.
